The best time to visit chicago is June, July and September, when temperatures are comfortable and rainfall is relatively low. Try to avoid March, April and December, when conditions are at their least comfortable. chicago experiences a wide temperature swing across the year, from a cold January averaging around 24.1F (-4.4C) to a hot July averaging around 74.5F (23.6C). In a warm July, average temperatures can reach 78.1F (25.6C), while a cold July may only average 70.9F (21.6C). Similarly, January averages range from 17.6F (-8.0C) in a cold year to 31.3F (-0.4C) in a mild one. Rainfall peaks in May and is lowest in February.
