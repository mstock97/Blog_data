Months to avoidFebruaryMarchSeptember

The best time to visit Tokyo is January, May and June, when temperatures are comfortable and rainfall is relatively low. Try to avoid February, March and September, when conditions are at their least comfortable. Tokyo experiences a wide temperature swing across the year, from a cold January averaging around 44.8F (7.1C) to a hot August averaging around 85.8F (29.9C). In a warm August, average temperatures can reach 85.8F (29.9C), while a cold August may only average 85.8F (29.9C). Similarly, January averages range from 44.2F (6.8C) in a cold year to 45.3F (7.4C) in a mild one. Rainfall peaks in October and is lowest in January.
